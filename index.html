ABOUT THE HEADCOUNT

The Headcount, is a real-time hosted Software-As-A-Service (SAAS) provider for monitoring labor compliance. The Headcount platform offers an array of modules to support clients who are required by rule (and good-faith) to monitor diversity workforce participation, wage-theft, ghost-hours and an array of other industry specific objectives and executive orders. The Headcount platform is a user-friendly, intuitive system that includes a number of reports that can be downloaded as an Excel or PDF document.
